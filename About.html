About


Our project "Deciphering Communication" is a collaborative work of three budding developers Aseem Shrey , Anurag Kumar Kushwaha , Ayush Bansal. The project includes a simple website providing information about communication in a lucid manner.

In this project we have pondered over different aspects of communication including it's history , and various subdivisions.The project highlights how the era of communication shifted from landline to mobile phones , from copper wires to optic fibres , from DSL to Wireless fidelity.We all have put sincere efforts in this project.
